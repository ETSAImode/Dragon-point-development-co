Inaugural Project
312 S Harbor City Blvd

Our first build is the new headquarters for HRTK Firearms—a purpose-built, 10,000 square foot flagship on Harbor City Boulevard in Melbourne. Built different. Built to perform. This isn't a leased office stitched into a strip center. It's a ground-up brand HQ designed for a manufacturer that takes its identity seriously.

Two stories of showroom, workshop, R&D, and offices, capped by a rooftop deck with skyline views of downtown Melbourne. Coastal-modern architecture, board-formed concrete, custom steel detailing, and an interior built around how serious gunsmiths and machinists actually work—not how a designer thought they should.
